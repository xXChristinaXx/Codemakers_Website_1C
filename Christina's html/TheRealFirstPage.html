We are sunrae, a restaraunt that takes pride in our fresh, pesticide free, locally

grown Canadian ingredents. Once every three months we choose a group of deserving charities

that we would be interested in sponsoring. The general public votes on which one charity

we should donate to. We donate 25% of our proceeds to these institutions.
